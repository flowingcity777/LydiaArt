Meet the Team

Architect Seung H-Sang

Born in 1952, Seung H-Sang is a leading South Korean architect who studied in Seoul and Vienna. After working with Kim Swoo Geun, he founded IROJE architects & planners in 1989 and was a key figure in the "4.3 Group," advocating for new architectural ideas. He helped establish the Seoul School of Architecture and has held numerous professorships internationally, currently at Dong-A University.

Interior Designer Ban Gi-Min

Ban GiMin is a prominent interior designer with a proven track record in shaping residential spaces. His portfolio includes significant contributions to apartment building projects, striking showroom designs, and engaging displays, all built upon his extensive experience with Hanssem Furniture and his personal venture, ViChe Design Studio.
